*Ice* is a dystopian novel by Anna Kavan and was first published in London in 1967 by Peter Owen. It was the third of Kavan's book published by Peter Owen, the other two being *Eagle's Nest* and *A Bright Green Field*. *Ice* was the last novel published before Kavan's death in 1968. When *Ice* was published, Kavan had not had much commercial success with her previous works, and it was only after her death (from heart failure) that the book attracted critical attention.

In this copy, the front pastedown contains the signature of a previous owner (M Turner?). The dust jacket is whitened from bumping and rubbing and shows general shelf wear.
